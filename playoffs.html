The top eight teams in each conference make the playoffs, with the top five seeds being the division champions and the other three being the top teams who did not win their division. The tie-breakers are as follows:

  1. Overall record
  2. Direct matchups
  3. Divisional record
  4. Total Points Scored
  5. Average Point Differential
